Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

Simply tap "Start" on the welcome screen to begin using the app. No account registration required!

How do I upgrade to PRO?

Go to Settings and tap "Upgrade to PRO". PRO is a one-time purchase ($1.99) that unlocks unlimited practice plans, unlimited history records, higher cultivation realms, and white noise features.

How do I restore my PRO purchase?

If you've previously purchased PRO, go to the PRO page and tap "Restore Purchase". Make sure you're using the same Apple ID that made the original purchase.

What are the differences between Free and PRO?

Free users can create 1 practice plan and store up to 10 history records. PRO users get unlimited practice plans, unlimited history records, access to higher cultivation realms, and white noise features.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes! All features work offline. Your data is stored locally on your device.

How do I delete my data?

Uninstalling the app will delete all local data. Note: This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
